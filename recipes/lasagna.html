Lasagna Recipe

Description

Recipe for the Brenda's lasagna. Looks pretty good

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes
  2. Preheat oven to 175C. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brwon beef and season with salt and pepper;drain
  3. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes
  4. In a medium bowl, combine the cheeses and stir well
  5. In a pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled
  6. Bake in over for 30 minutes
