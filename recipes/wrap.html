Wrap

Description

Recipe for a wrap with a v2 burger

Ingredients

  • One tortilla
  • One v2 burger
  • One cup of gardan salad
  • Salt and pepper to tase
  • Chilli sauce to tast

Steps

  1. Cook the v2 burger for 5 mins either side on a medium heat pan
  2. Cut the v2 burger into slices
  3. Heat the tortilla in the microwave for 25 seconds
  4. Assemble all the items in the middle of the tortilla
  5. Wrap the tortilla and enjoy
